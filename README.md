# "The Book." 

A cosmogony in the style of Gnostic esoteric scripture based on modern cosmology, 
theories of abiogenesis and neuroscience. The Book will trace history of the universe from
[before?] the Big Bang, through the initial cooling with the phase changes at the separation of 
energy and matter as well as the breaking of matter/antimatter symmetry. It will include the 
condensation of matter to form stellar objects on the "ripples" of the earliest time. 
Then from inanimate to animate from modern thearies of abiogenesis. When life exists there is 
the evolution from taxis to sensing and agency leading to self-awareness and consciousness. 

All of this is in accordance with the laws of nature. The mystery is how these laws which have
no substance, will or agency can "govern" everything in existence. These maybe equated the Aeons.
it is important to avoid anthropomorphism and attributing agency to these.

The philosophy underpinning this book can be described as:

- **Monistic**: Embracing the oneness of all that exists, rejecting dualistic interpretations.
- **Deterministic**: Recognizing that all events are caused, and everything follows a logical flow of causality.
- **Acknowledging Free Will**: Affirming the reality of free will within the deterministic framework.
